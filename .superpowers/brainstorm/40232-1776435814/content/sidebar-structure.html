How should the Admin sub-navigation work?

The sidebar has two top-level sections: Generate and Admin. Admin has 7 sub-pages. Pick the structure that feels right for this internal tool.

A

Always-expanded flat list

All 7 admin links are always visible. Simple, predictable, no interaction needed.
