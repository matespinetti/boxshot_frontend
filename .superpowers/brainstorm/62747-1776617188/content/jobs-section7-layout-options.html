A. History page + full review page

/jobs is a compact jobs index. Clicking a row opens a dedicated review workspace at /jobs/[id].

Pros

  • Clear route separation
  • Best fit for current redirect flow after Generate
  • Lower implementation risk

Cons

  • Two pages to maintain
  • Less “power-user” density

B. Unified jobs workspace

/jobs is the main workspace with a list on the left and the selected job on the right. /jobs/[id] can deep-link into the same view.

Pros

  • Fast switching between jobs
  • High-density operations UI

Cons

  • Heavier first implementation
  • More state syncing between list and detail
  • Less aligned with the current redirect target
